The Wislandic word for money, like as a concept, is *Alkoeh*. Alkoeh is also the generic term for all currencies for example: (*Amerlan Alkoeh* would mean USD). But the official currency of Wisland is collectively called the *Alki*, after the base coin.

The Wislandic Alki is not decimalized, and there can be no fractional amount, at least in practice. It is composed of metal coins, not paper bills, but letters of credit from the merchants guild are often used as well. A letter of credit is basically a bond from the guild that can be redeemed at any guild for the amount paid for the bond. There are 3 coin tiers, with 2 sub coins, so 5 distinct coins total.

## Alki

The Alki is the base coin, made of bonze

## Tohlki

The Tohlki or Tolh Alki, is equal to twelve Alkis. It is also 1/6th of a shiftohn. It is made of bronze like the Alki, but is slightly larger.
